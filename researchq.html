Research Questions

1. How has the addition of lighthouses affected the severity or rate of shipwrecks?
2. In what way does a ship’s route change based on size/value of cargo?
3. How has the advancement of technology contributed to the number of shipwrecks?
4. What trade routes result in more shipwrecks?
5. What effect have shipwrecks had on the surrounding ecosystem?
6. In what ways did globalization increase traffic along maritime routes and did this result in more shipwrecks?
7. How did industrialization affect the ways ships were built to sustain maritime travel?
8. Did geographic variations in the oceanic basin in the Atlantic (Atlantic basin) change over time and could it be the cause of any shipwrecks?
9. Are there any correlations between different weather conditions and the shipwrecks?
10.Do the amount of shipwrecks in proximity to a given port city correlate with the level of commerce in that port city?
11. How did the nature of the cargo affect the materials used to build the vessels over time as technology advanced?
12. How did the industrial revolution affect trade routes and importation of cargo?
13. In what ways have artificial reefs contributed to shipwrecks or changed the routes of vessels?
14. How did the amount of shipwrecks in a given time span affect the amount of traffic of a port?
15. How has the modernization of cargo vessels contributed to the amount of shipwrecks near New Jersey in the 20th century?
